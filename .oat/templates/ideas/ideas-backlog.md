# Ideas Backlog

Captured ideas from brainstorming sessions. Each idea has a full discovery document in its own directory under `.oat/ideas/`.

## How to Use

- New ideas start in **Active Brainstorming** (created by `oat-idea-new`)
- When summarized, they move to **Captured Ideas** (via `oat-idea-summarize`)
- Archive completed, abandoned, or promoted ideas to **Archived**
